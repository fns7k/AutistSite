Services & Therapies

Integrated supports designed around strengths, goals, and daily routines.

Speech & Language Therapy

Communication, social interaction, augmentative and alternative communication (AAC), and feeding support. Play-based sessions with visual supports and caregiver coaching.

Occupational Therapy

Sensory processing, regulation, fine and gross motor skills, daily living skills (ADLs), and environmental adaptations to support participation.

Behavioral & Skills Coaching

Functional, goal-focused strategies to build adaptive skills and independence while honoring autonomy and preferences.

Psychology

Assessment, anxiety supports, social-emotional learning, and collaborative plans with educators and community services.

Parent & Caregiver Training

Coaching and resources so you can support progress between sessions in natural contexts.

School & Community Partnerships

Consultation for teachers and programs, individualized support plans, and professional development.
