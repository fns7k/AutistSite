Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about services, assessments, scheduling, and funding.

How do we start services?

Begin with an intake consultation where we learn your goals and recommend supports.

Do you provide assessments?

Yes. Our clinicians provide developmental, speech/language, OT, and psychological assessments.

What ages do you see?

We support children and young people from infancy through adolescence.

How do sessions work?

Sessions are individualized. We coach caregivers and collaborate with schools to support progress in daily routines.

Still have questions?

Reach out and our care coordinator will help you.
